N Performance

Another face of N, beyond limits.

  • iONIQ 5N
    DK Edition     An N Performance parts tuning package perfected with the driving expertise of ‘Drift King’ Keiichi Tsuchiya.

  • Adding the spirit of the Drift King to the ultimate high-performance EV, the IONIQ 5 N.
    Experience the joy of everyday driving with the DK Edition performance parts, developed in collaboration with Keiichi Tsuchiya.

  • The IONIQ 5 N DK Edition, developed in collaboration with “Drift King” Keiichi Tsuchiya, is engineered to deliver stable performance under any driving conditions. With 21-inch lightweight forged wheels, a 6P monoblock brake system, and optimized lowering springs, it offers enhanced control and stability even in dynamic environments. Lightweight carbon parts, including the front splitter, rear wing spoiler, side skirts, and rear diffuser, maximize downforce and improve grip and high-speed stability. As a result, the DK Edition goes beyond the limits of high-performance EVs to deliver an ultimate driving experience on both track and road.

  • From motorsport to everyday life, a unique creation shaped by TCR-winning expertise.
    The Avante N TCR Edition is refined from TCR racing car technology, undergoing relentless testing and tuning to meet Hyundai N’s highest standards.

  • Every detail you see and touch is filled with the unique character of the TCR Edition.

  • IONIQ 5 N NPX1 Unveiled at the 2024 Tokyo Auto Salon, “NPX1” is the first concept model based on the award-winning high-performance electric vehicle, the IONIQ 5 N.

  • The “NPX1” concept previews various N-exclusive parts coming to future N Performance Parts. It features carbon aero components, lightweight wheels, high-performance brakes, and lowering springs, while the interior is refined with Alcantara and metal door scuff plates.
