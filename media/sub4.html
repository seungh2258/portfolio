Rolling Lab

Innovation in Action

Rolling Lab is Hyundai N’s experimental vehicle program that tests and develops cutting-edge technologies in real driving environments, bridging motorsport experience with production car innovation.

  • N 2025 Vision Gran Turismo

    The Hyundai N 2025 Vision Gran Turismo, created to celebrate Hyundai Motor Company’s 50th anniversary in 2017, perfectly embodies the passion behind the high-performance technologies applied to the N brand.

  • N Vision 74

    Born from seven years of research, the high-performance hydrogen hybrid N Vision 74 demonstrates Hyundai N’s commitment to pushing beyond the limits of electric vehicles and exploring new possibilities.

  • RN 24

    The RN24 is a rolling lab that combines over a decade of WRC (World Rally Championship) expertise with advanced technologies developed through the high-performance EV, IONIQ 5 N. It represents the culmination of Hyundai N’s next-generation electrification vision.

  • RN 22e

    The RN22e is the first E-GMP-based rolling lab that opens the door to the realization of EV N.

  • RM 20e

    The RM20e’s electric powertrain not only achieves zero emissions but also delivers the thrilling power and instant torque of a race car. Sustainability and exhilarating driving can coexist.

  • RM 19

    RM stands for the Racing Midship layout of N prototype models. This configuration delivers supercar-level handling balance and agility through a low polar moment of inertia, providing exceptional grip at all speeds and driving conditions — a key element that sets it apart.
